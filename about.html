About Don Odyssey Safaris

Founded in 2010, Don Odyssey Safaris began as a small team of passionate guides with a shared love for Tanzania's natural wonders. What started as a humble operation has grown into one of Tanzania's most respected tour operators, known for our exceptional service, expert guides, and commitment to sustainable tourism.

Our mission is simple: to provide unforgettable experiences while preserving Tanzania's incredible biodiversity and supporting local communities. We believe that responsible tourism can be a force for good, creating meaningful connections between travelers and the places they visit.

With over a decade of experience, we've perfected our itineraries to showcase the very best of Tanzania while ensuring the highest standards of safety, comfort, and environmental responsibility.
