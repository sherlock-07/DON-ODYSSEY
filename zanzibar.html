Zanzibar FAQs

Answers to common questions about Zanzibar

Zanzibar is warm year-round, but the best weather is during the dry seasons from June-October and December-February. The "long rains" (March-May) can bring heavy showers, while the "short rains" (November) are usually brief afternoon showers.

Zanzibar is generally very safe for tourists. Standard precautions apply - don't leave valuables unattended, avoid isolated areas at night, and follow local customs (modest dress is appreciated outside beach areas). Our team provides 24/7 support during your stay.
