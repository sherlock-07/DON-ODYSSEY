Arusha: Gateway to Tanzania's Wildlife

Arusha is the perfect starting point for your Tanzanian safari adventure. Located in northern Tanzania, this vibrant city provides easy access to the country's most famous national parks and conservation areas.

With Don Odyssey Safaris departing from Arusha, you'll experience:

  • Convenient departure from Arusha city or airport
  • Short transfer times to northern circuit parks
  • Pre-safari briefings at our Arusha office
  • Flexible start times to suit your schedule
  • Options to combine with Kilimanjaro climbs
  • Post-safari relaxation in Arusha's finest lodges

Why Start Your Safari from Arusha?

Discover the advantages of beginning your Tanzanian adventure in the safari capital

Strategic Location

Arusha is centrally located for easy access to Tanzania's northern safari circuit, minimizing travel time between parks.

Complete Services

Find all necessary safari services in Arusha, from equipment rental to expert guides and comfortable accommodations.

Kilimanjaro Views

Enjoy stunning views of Mount Kilimanjaro from Arusha on clear days, adding to your African experience.

Safari Preparation

Use Arusha as your base to purchase any last-minute items, exchange currency, and attend pre-safari briefings.

Flexible Itineraries

Multiple route options from Arusha allow for customized itineraries based on your interests and time available.

Local Culture

Experience the vibrant culture of Arusha with its markets, restaurants, and opportunities to interact with local communities.
