Oldonyo Lengai Experience

1-2 Days 2,962m 80% Success Rate

Known as the "Mountain of God" to the Maasai, Oldonyo Lengai (2,962m/9,718ft) is Africa's only active volcano that erupts natrocarbonatite lava. This unique night climb offers an unforgettable adventure with sunrise views over the Rift Valley.

Difficulty Level:
Easy Moderate Challenging Strenuous
Location

Northern Tanzania, near Lake Natron

Best Time to Climb

June-October (cooler temperatures)

Climb Type

Night ascent, technical scramble

Why Climb Lengai With Us

Night Ascent Expertise

Our guides specialize in the unique challenges of night climbs with proper lighting and safety measures.

Volcanic Knowledge

Learn about the unique carbonatite lava from guides trained in volcanic geology.

Lake Natron Combo

Most packages include visits to Lake Natron's flamingo colonies and waterfalls.

Cultural Respect

We follow proper protocols for this sacred Maasai mountain with local guides.
