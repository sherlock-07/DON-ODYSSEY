Safari Overview

Embark on a breathtaking 2-day safari through the heart of Tanzania, starting from Arusha. Experience the magnificent wildlife and stunning landscapes of Tarangire National Park and the iconic Ngorongoro Crater. In Tarangire, you'll marvel at the large elephant herds and ancient baobab trees, while Ngorongoro promises encounters with the Big Five and unparalleled scenic beauty. This adventure is perfect for those looking to immerse themselves in nature and witness Tanzania's incredible biodiversity in a short amount of time.

Detailed Itinerary

Day-by-day breakdown of your Tarangire & Ngorongoro safari adventure

Your safari adventure begins with a warm welcome as we pick you up from your hotel in Arusha or Kilimanjaro. Our safari guide will provide a brief overview of the exciting journey ahead. You will then embark on a scenic two-hour drive to Tarangire National Park (three-hour drive if your hotel is in Kilimanjaro). If you need any last-minute items, feel free to ask your guide to stop at a supermarket along the way. Otherwise, we will head straight to the park.

Upon arrival, your guide will take care of the necessary permits, and you will dive right into thrilling game drives. Tarangire is famous for its large elephant herds, and you can also expect to see majestic lions, elusive leopards, zebras, warthogs, wildebeests, monkeys, and elegant giraffes. For bird enthusiasts, the park is a paradise with a wide variety of bird species to spot.

Around noon, enjoy a delightful picnic lunch surrounded by the park's stunning scenery. After lunch, continue with more exhilarating game drives, exploring the diverse landscapes and abundant wildlife until evening. As the day winds down, we will transfer you to your accommodation, where you can relax and savor a delicious dinner before settling in for a restful overnight stay.

Key Features:
  • Scenic drive from Arusha to Tarangire National Park
  • Game drives focusing on elephant herds and diverse wildlife
  • Picnic lunch in the heart of the park
  • Bird watching opportunities with numerous species

On the second and final day of your remarkable adventure, you will start with an early morning breakfast at your accommodation. Our professional guide will then pick you up for a scenic drive to the 8th wonder of the world, the Ngorongoro Crater. Along the way, you will pass through charming towns like Karatu and see Maasai villages nestled in the Ngorongoro Highlands before entering the crater.

Upon arrival at the Ngorongoro gate, your guide will handle all the necessary permits, and you will dive straight into thrilling game drives. Expect to see the iconic Big Five: lions, elephants, buffalo, leopards, and the elusive black rhino. If you are lucky, you might even see them all! In addition to the Big Five, you may encounter other fascinating wildlife such as hyenas, zebras, wildebeest, elands, antelopes, and a variety of bird species.

Game drives will continue until the afternoon, when you will head for a picnic lunch near the hippo pool. Here, you can enjoy your meal while taking in the sights and sounds of these magnificent creatures in their natural habitat. After lunch, the adventure continues with more game drives, allowing you to spot even more wildlife as you explore the crater.

As the evening approaches, you will drive out of the crater and head back to Arusha or to the airport, carrying with you unforgettable memories of this incredible journey. Please note that the time of exiting the crater can vary depending on the distance to your hotel or if you have an early evening flight on the last day.

Key Features:
  • Explore the Ngorongoro Crater, a UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • Opportunity to spot the Big Five in their natural habitat
  • Picnic lunch near the hippo pool with scenic views
  • Visit Maasai villages in the Ngorongoro Highlands
