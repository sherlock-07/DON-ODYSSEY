3-Day Safari Overview

Embark on an unforgettable 3-day safari adventure in Tanzania, beginning from Arusha. Journey through the captivating landscapes and rich wildlife of Tarangire National Park, Ngorongoro Crater, and Lake Manyara. This safari offers a perfect blend of thrilling wildlife encounters and breathtaking vistas, ideal for those seeking an immersive Tanzanian experience in a short timeframe.

  • Witness large elephant herds in Tarangire
  • Spot tree-climbing lions in Lake Manyara
  • Diverse ecosystems and landscapes
  • Professional guides and comfortable vehicles

Detailed Itinerary

Day-by-day breakdown of your 3-day safari adventure

Your safari adventure begins with a warm welcome as we pick you up from your hotel in Arusha or Kilimanjaro. Our safari guide will provide a brief overview of the exciting journey ahead. You will then embark on a scenic two-hour drive to Tarangire National Park (three-hour drive if your hotel is in Kilimanjaro).

Upon arrival, your guide will take care of the necessary permits, and you will dive right into thrilling game drives. Tarangire is famous for its large elephant herds, and you can also expect to see majestic lions, elusive leopards, zebras, warthogs, wildebeests, monkeys, and elegant giraffes. For bird enthusiasts, the park is a paradise with a wide variety of bird species to spot.

Around noon, enjoy a delightful picnic lunch surrounded by the park's stunning scenery. After lunch, continue with more exhilarating game drives, exploring the diverse landscapes and abundant wildlife until evening. As the day winds down, we will transfer you to your accommodation, where you can relax and savor a delicious dinner before settling in for a restful overnight stay.

Accommodation Options:
  • Budget: Ngare Lodge
  • Midrange: Heart and Soul Lodge
  • Luxury: The Retreat at Ngorongoro

On the second day of your remarkable adventure, you will start with an early morning breakfast at your accommodation. Our professional guide will then pick you up for a scenic drive to the 8th wonder of the world, the Ngorongoro Crater. Along the way, you will pass through charming towns like Karatu and see Maasai villages nestled in the Ngorongoro Highlands before entering the crater.

Upon arrival at the Ngorongoro gate, your guide will handle all the necessary permits, and you will dive straight into thrilling game drives. Expect to see the iconic Big Five: lions, elephants, buffalo, leopards, and the elusive black rhino. If you are lucky, you might even see them all! In addition to the Big Five, you may encounter other fascinating wildlife such as hyenas, zebras, wildebeest, elands, antelopes, and a variety of bird species.

Game drives will continue until the afternoon when you will head for a picnic lunch near the hippo pool. Here, you can enjoy your meal while taking in the sights and sounds of these magnificent creatures in their natural habitat. After lunch, the adventure continues with more game drives, allowing you to spot even more wildlife as you explore the crater.

As the evening approaches, you will drive out of the crater and head to your accommodation, where you can relax, enjoy dinner, and rest for the night preparing for the following day adventures.

Accommodation Options:
  • Budget: Ngare Lodge
  • Midrange: Heart and Soul Lodge
  • Luxury: Lake Manyara Kilimamoja Lodge

On the final day of your safari, you will start with a hearty breakfast at your accommodation. Our guide will pick you up for a short drive to Lake Manyara National Park.

Upon arrival, your guide will handle the necessary permits, and you will embark on a day filled with game drives. Lake Manyara is famous for its tree-climbing lions, diverse birdlife, and beautiful scenery. You can expect to see elephants, hippos, giraffes, zebras, and a variety of antelopes. Around noon, enjoy a picnic lunch in the park, taking in the serene surroundings. After lunch, continue your game drives, capturing more incredible moments and wildlife sightings.

As the afternoon progresses, you will start your journey back to Arusha. Depending on your schedule, you will be dropped off at your hotel in Arusha or transferred to the airport for your departure. Your safari adventure concludes with unforgettable memories and remarkable experiences of Tanzania's wild beauty.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our 3-day safari

On this 3-day safari, you can expect to see a wide variety of wildlife. In Tarangire, you'll encounter large elephant herds, lions, leopards, zebras, wildebeests, and giraffes. In Ngorongoro Crater, you have an excellent chance of seeing the Big Five (lion, elephant, buffalo, leopard, and rhino). Lake Manyara is famous for its tree-climbing lions and diverse birdlife.

This safari operates year-round, but the best wildlife viewing is during the dry seasons from June to October and January to February. The wet seasons (November-December and March-May) offer lush landscapes and fewer crowds, though some roads may be more challenging.

We recommend packing light, neutral-colored clothing, a warm jacket for early mornings and evenings, comfortable walking shoes, a hat, sunglasses, sunscreen, binoculars, camera with extra batteries, and any personal medications. Don't forget your passport and travel documents!
