Mount Meru Experience

3-4 Days 4,562m 85% Success Rate

At 4,562 meters (14,968 feet), Mount Meru is Tanzania's second highest peak and an excellent preparation climb for Kilimanjaro. Located in Arusha National Park, it offers stunning views of Kilimanjaro and unique wildlife encounters during the ascent.

Difficulty Level:
Easy Moderate Challenging Strenuous
Location

Arusha National Park, northern Tanzania

Best Time to Climb

June-February (avoid heavy rains in April-May)

Wildlife

Possible sightings of buffalo, giraffe, and antelope

Why Climb Meru With Us

Kilimanjaro Prep

Perfect acclimatization climb with similar terrain but shorter duration than Kili.

Wildlife Views

Unique opportunity to see wildlife while mountain climbing in Africa.

Less Crowded

Far fewer climbers than Kilimanjaro for a more peaceful experience.

Great Value

More affordable than Kilimanjaro with spectacular views.

Momella Route Itinerary

4-day standard ascent with wildlife viewing

1

Momella Gate to Miriakamba Hut

Elevation: 1,500m to 2,500m
Hike Time: 4-6 hours
Habitat: Montane Forest

After registration, hike through forest where you might see buffalo, giraffe and antelope.

2

Miriakamba Hut to Saddle Hut

Elevation: 2,500m to 3,570m
Hike Time: 3-5 hours
Habitat: Moorland

Steeper ascent through open moorland to Saddle Hut below Little Meru.

3

Summit Day: Socialist Peak

Elevation: 3,570m to 4,562m (then descend to 2,500m)
Hike Time: 10-12 hours
Habitat: Alpine Desert to Summit

Early morning start for sunrise summit. Spectacular views of Kilimanjaro.

4

Miriakamba Hut to Momella Gate

Elevation: 2,500m to 1,500m
Hike Time: 2-3 hours

Gradual descent through different vegetation zones. Return to Arusha.

Summit Direct Itinerary

3-day fast ascent for experienced climbers

1

Momella Gate to Saddle Hut

Elevation: 1,500m to 3,570m
Hike Time: 6-8 hours
Habitat: Forest to Moorland

Long day ascending directly to Saddle Hut with minimal stops.

2

Summit Day: Socialist Peak

Elevation: 3,570m to 4,562m (then descend to 2,500m)
Hike Time: 10-12 hours

Early morning summit push, then descend to Miriakamba Hut.

3

Miriakamba Hut to Momella Gate

Elevation: 2,500m to 1,500m
Hike Time: 2-3 hours

Final descent through forest to gate.
