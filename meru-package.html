Mount Meru Experience

3-4 Days 4,562m 85% Success Rate

At 4,562 meters (14,968 feet), Mount Meru is Tanzania's second highest peak and an excellent preparation climb for Kilimanjaro. Located in Arusha National Park, it offers stunning views of Kilimanjaro and unique wildlife encounters during the ascent.

Difficulty Level:
Easy Moderate Challenging Strenuous
Location

Arusha National Park, northern Tanzania

Best Time to Climb

June-February (avoid heavy rains in April-May)

Wildlife

Possible sightings of buffalo, giraffe, and antelope

Why Climb Meru With Us

Kilimanjaro Prep

Perfect acclimatization climb with similar terrain but shorter duration than Kili.

Wildlife Views

Unique opportunity to see wildlife while mountain climbing in Africa.

Less Crowded

Far fewer climbers than Kilimanjaro for a more peaceful experience.

Great Value

More affordable than Kilimanjaro with spectacular views.

Trekking FAQs

Answers to common questions about our mountain adventures

Kilimanjaro: Moderate difficulty but requires endurance. Train with 4-6 hour hikes carrying 5kg pack for 2+ months.

Mount Meru: Good for intermediates. Focus on stair climbing and leg strength.

Oldonyo Lengai: Technically challenging - requires scrambling experience. Train on steep, uneven terrain.

All climbs benefit from cardiovascular training and altitude hikes if possible.

Our protocols exceed industry standards:
  • Daily health checks with pulse oximeters
  • "Pole pole" (slow) pace for proper acclimatization
  • Extra acclimatization days on Kilimanjaro routes
  • Emergency oxygen and Gamow bags on all climbs
  • Immediate descent protocol for severe AMS
