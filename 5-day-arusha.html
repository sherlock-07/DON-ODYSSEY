Safari Overview

Dive into an epic 5-day adventure exploring Tanzania's iconic wildlife and stunning landscapes. Embark on an extraordinary safari adventure through Tanzania's most iconic destinations: Tarangire National Park, Serengeti National Park, and Ngorongoro Crater.

Start your journey in Tarangire, where you will marvel at large elephant herds and majestic baobab trees. Continue to the world-renowned Serengeti, experiencing its vast plains teeming with diverse wildlife, including lions, elephants, and zebras. Conclude your adventure with a visit to the awe-inspiring Ngorongoro Crater, home to the Big Five and offering breathtaking landscapes.

Enjoy thrilling game drives, picturesque picnics, and immersive wildlife encounters, making this safari the perfect choice for those seeking an unforgettable Tanzanian adventure.

Detailed Itinerary

Day-by-day breakdown of your 5-day Tanzania Safari adventure

Your safari adventure begins with a warm welcome as we pick you up from your hotel in Arusha or Kilimanjaro. Our safari guide will provide a brief overview of the exciting journey ahead. You will then embark on a scenic two-hour drive to Tarangire National Park (three-hour drive if your hotel is in Kilimanjaro).

If you need any last-minute items, feel free to ask your guide to stop at a supermarket along the way. Otherwise, we will head straight to the park.

Upon arrival, your guide will take care of the necessary permits, and you will dive right into thrilling game drives. Tarangire is famous for its large elephant herds, and you can also expect to see majestic lions, elusive leopards, zebras, warthogs, wildebeests, monkeys, and elegant giraffes. For bird enthusiasts, the park is a paradise with a wide variety of bird species to spot.

Around noon, enjoy a delightful picnic lunch surrounded by the park's stunning scenery. After lunch, continue with more exhilarating game drives, exploring the diverse landscapes and abundant wildlife until evening.

As the day winds down, we will transfer you to your accommodation, where you can relax and savor a delicious dinner before settling in for a restful overnight stay.

Key Features:
  • Pickup from Arusha or Kilimanjaro
  • Game drives in Tarangire National Park
  • Large elephant herds and baobab trees
  • Picnic lunch in the park

The next day starts with a hearty breakfast at your lodge, fueling you for an adventurous day ahead. Our professional guide will meet you after breakfast, and you will embark on a picturesque journey to Serengeti National Park.

The drive takes you through the charming town of Karatu, where you might catch a glimpse of the local market life. Continuing on, you will traverse the scenic Ngorongoro Highlands, with rolling green hills and vast landscapes that make for a mesmerizing backdrop.

As you approach the Serengeti, the excitement builds. Upon arrival, your guide will take care of the necessary permits while you stretch your legs and enjoy a picnic lunch amidst the stunning surroundings. The Serengeti's vast plains stretch out before you, hinting at the incredible wildlife encounters that await.

After lunch, it's time to dive into the heart of the Serengeti with thrilling game drives. The Park is renowned for its abundance of wildlife. You can expect to see majestic lions lazing in the sun, herds of elephants meandering through the grasslands, and zebras and wildebeests grazing peacefully. Keep your eyes peeled for the elusive leopards that often find refuge in the park's rocky outcrops.

Bird enthusiasts will be in paradise as the Serengeti is home to a diverse array of bird species. From the strikingly colorful lilac-breasted roller to the imposing martial eagle, there's no shortage of avian wonders to behold.

As you drive through the park, your guide will share insights about the animals and the ecosystem, enriching your understanding of this magnificent landscape.

As the sun begins to set, casting a golden hue over the plains, you will head to your accommodation nestled within the park. Here, you will be greeted with a warm welcome and a delicious dinner. After an exhilarating day of wildlife viewing, you will have the chance to relax and unwind in the comfort of your lodge, reflecting on the day's adventures and preparing for another day of exploration.

Key Features:
  • Scenic drive through Ngorongoro Highlands
  • Game drives in Serengeti National Park
  • Diverse wildlife including lions, elephants, and leopards
  • Bird watching opportunities

The third day begins with an early breakfast at your lodge, preparing you for a full day of exploration in the Serengeti. Our professional guide will pick you up for an entire day dedicated to game drives, giving you ample opportunity to witness the incredible wildlife and landscapes that make the Serengeti so special.

In the early morning hours, the wildlife is particularly active. Watch as predators like lions and cheetahs begin their hunt. Herds of wildebeests and zebras graze in the open plains, and elephants gather near water sources. The Serengeti's endless horizon provides a stunning backdrop for photography enthusiasts.

Around midday, you will break for lunch at a scenic spot in the park. After lunch, continue exploring different regions of the Serengeti, such as the Seronera Valley, known for its high density of leopards and lions, or venture to the Moru Kopjes, where you might spot the rare black rhino.

As the day draws to a close, you will return to your accommodation for dinner and overnight stay, reflecting on the day's incredible experiences and perhaps sharing stories around a campfire under the star-studded African sky.

Key Features:
  • Full day game drives in Serengeti
  • Early morning wildlife viewing
  • Explore Seronera Valley and Moru Kopjes
  • Photography opportunities

The next day begins with an early breakfast at your lodge, preparing you for another thrilling day of exploration. Our professional guide will then pick you up for more exciting game drives in the Serengeti. As the sun rises, casting a golden glow over the plains, you will set off in search of the park's incredible wildlife.

Throughout the morning, you will traverse the vast landscapes, encountering various animals in their natural habitat. Watch as lions bask in the early morning sun, herds of wildebeest graze peacefully, and elephants make their way through the bush. The diverse ecosystems of the Serengeti provide endless opportunities for fascinating wildlife sightings.

Around midday, you will break for lunch, enjoying a picnic in the heart of the Serengeti. Take this time to relax and soak in the breathtaking surroundings while savoring your meal. After lunch, the adventure continues with a transfer game drive, during which you will still have the chance to spot various animals as you make your way through the park.

As the afternoon progresses, you will begin your journey out of the Serengeti, but the excitement doesn't end there. Keep your eyes peeled for any last-minute wildlife encounters as you traverse the park's diverse terrains. From the grasslands to the woodlands, the Serengeti is full of surprises and incredible sights.

In the evening, you will arrive at your next accommodation, where a warm welcome awaits. Unwind and relax with a delightful dinner, sharing stories of the day's adventures. After a fulfilling day of wildlife exploration, you will settle in for a restful overnight stay, recharging for the final day of your safari journey.

Key Features:
  • Morning game drives in Serengeti
  • Transfer to Ngorongoro Conservation Area
  • Last wildlife encounters in Serengeti
  • New accommodation near Ngorongoro

On the final day of your remarkable adventure, you will start with an early morning breakfast at your accommodation. Our professional guide will then pick you up for a scenic drive to the 8th wonder of the world, the Ngorongoro Crater.

Along the way, you will pass through charming towns like Karatu and see Maasai villages nestled in the Ngorongoro Highlands before entering the crater.

Upon arrival at the Ngorongoro gate, your guide will handle all the necessary permits, and you will dive straight into thrilling game drives. Expect to see the iconic Big Five: lions, elephants, buffalo, leopards, and the elusive black rhino. If you are lucky, you might even see them all!

In addition to the Big Five, you may encounter other fascinating wildlife such as hyenas, zebras, wildebeest, elands, antelopes, and a variety of bird species. Game drives will continue until the afternoon, when you will head for a picnic lunch near the hippo pool. Here, you can enjoy your meal while taking in the sights and sounds of these magnificent creatures in their natural habitat.

After lunch, the adventure continues with more game drives, allowing you to spot even more wildlife as you explore the crater.

As the evening approaches, you will drive out of the crater and head back to Arusha or to the airport, carrying with you unforgettable memories of this incredible journey. Please note that the time of exiting the crater can vary depending on the distance to your hotel or if you have an early evening flight on the last day.

Key Features:
  • Game drives in Ngorongoro Crater
  • Big Five wildlife viewing
  • Picnic lunch near hippo pool
  • Return to Arusha or airport

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our 5-Day Tanzania Safari

This safari operates year-round. The dry season (June-October) offers the best wildlife viewing as animals gather around water sources. The wet season (November-May) features lush landscapes, fewer tourists, and is excellent for bird watching. The Great Migration can be witnessed in the Serengeti typically from December-July.

We recommend neutral-colored clothing (khaki, green, beige), comfortable walking shoes, a wide-brimmed hat, sunglasses, sunscreen, binoculars, camera with extra batteries, a light jacket for cooler mornings and evenings, and any personal medications. A detailed packing list will be provided upon booking.

Yes, this safari is suitable for children aged 6 and above. The game drives are conducted in comfortable vehicles, and the pace can be adjusted to accommodate families. We recommend discussing any specific needs with our team when booking.
