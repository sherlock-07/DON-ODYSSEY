Safari FAQs

Answers to common questions about our safaris

The best wildlife viewing is during the dry seasons (June-October and December-February). The Great Migration is typically in Serengeti from December-July, with river crossings in the north around August-September. January-February is calving season in the southern Serengeti.

Essentials include neutral-colored clothing, a wide-brimmed hat, sturdy walking shoes, binoculars, sunscreen, a camera with zoom lens, and light layers for cool mornings. We provide a detailed packing list upon booking.
